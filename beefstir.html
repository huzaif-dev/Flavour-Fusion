Authentic Beef Stir-Fry

A quick and flavorful stir-fry with tender beef and crisp veggies.

Ingredients

  • 400g beef strips
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp ginger

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and stir-fry beef until browned.
  2. Add vegetables and ginger, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
