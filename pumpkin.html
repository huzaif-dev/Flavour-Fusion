Authentic Pumpkin Soup

A warm and creamy soup made with roasted pumpkin and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 small pumpkin, roasted
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion until soft.
  2. Add roasted pumpkin and stock, simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Blend until smooth, then stir in cream and nutmeg.
  4. Heat through and serve.
