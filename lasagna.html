Authentic Roasted Vegetable Lasagna

A layered lasagna with roasted veggies and rich cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups roasted vegetables (zucchini, eggplant)
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles until al dente.
  2. Layer noodles, veggies, ricotta, and sauce in a dish.
  3. Top with mozzarella.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let cool and serve.
