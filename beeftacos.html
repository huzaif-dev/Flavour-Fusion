Authentic Beef Tacos

Spicy and savory beef tacos served with fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 tomato, diced

Instructions

  1. Cook beef with taco seasoning until browned.
  2. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  3. Fill shells with beef, lettuce, and tomato.
  4. Add optional toppings like cheese or salsa.
  5. Serve immediately.
