Authentic Eggplant Parmesan

A vegetarian twist on the classic with breaded eggplant and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Dip eggplant slices in egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry until golden, then layer in a dish with sauce and cheese.
  3. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
  4. Let cool slightly and serve.
