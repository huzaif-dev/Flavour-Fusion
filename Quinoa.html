Authentic Quinoa Salad

A light and healthy salad with quinoa, veggies, and a tangy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Combine quinoa with cucumber and bell pepper.
  3. Dress with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Toss and chill before serving.
