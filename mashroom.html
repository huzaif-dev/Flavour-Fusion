Authentic Mushroom Risotto

A creamy and earthy risotto made with mushrooms and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and mushrooms until soft.
  2. Add rice and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Gradually add broth, stirring until absorbed.
  4. Stir in Parmesan and serve hot.
