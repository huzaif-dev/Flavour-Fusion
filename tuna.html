Authentic Tuna Casserole

A comforting casserole with tuna, noodles, and creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles until al dente.
  2. Mix noodles, tuna, soup, peas, and half the cheese.
  3. Transfer to a baking dish and top with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve warm.
