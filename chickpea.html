Authentic Chickpea Curry

A spicy and creamy curry made with chickpeas and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (cooked)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and curry powder, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer for 15 minutes and serve with rice.
