Authentic Lentil Soup

A hearty and nutritious soup made with lentils and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Rinse lentils and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion, carrots, and garlic in a pot.
  3. Add lentils and broth, bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until lentils are soft.
  5. Season and serve hot.
