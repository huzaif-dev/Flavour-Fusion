Authentic Chicken Parmesan

A crispy breaded chicken cutlet topped with marinara and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Beat egg and dip chicken in it, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry chicken until golden, then place in a baking dish.
  3. Top with marinara and cheese.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve with pasta.
